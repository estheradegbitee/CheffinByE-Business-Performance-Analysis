# CheffinBye Business Performance Analysis

## Project Overview

This project analyses the performance of CheffinBye, a pop-up Nigerian catering business based in Maynooth, Ireland. The objective was to transform raw sales data into meaningful business insights using Microsoft Excel.

The analysis focuses on revenue, profitability, customer retention, product performance and ordering channels to support data-driven business decisions.

---

## Business Problem

The business relied on manual tracking of customer orders, making it difficult to monitor performance, identify trends and make informed decisions.

This project demonstrates how Excel dashboards and business analysis techniques can be used to improve operational reporting.

---

## Objectives

- Analyse approximately 300 customer orders
- Measure revenue and profitability
- Identify best-selling products
- Analyse customer retention
- Evaluate ordering channels
- Produce an executive dashboard
